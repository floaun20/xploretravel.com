Top tourist attractions in Kenya

Kenya is a beautiful country that is gifted with a unique blend of nature,
warm sandy beaches and wildlife. No matter your taste you will
find your desired destination at the heart of Kenya.
Here are some of the top sites in Kenya:

Hell's Gate


The hells gate was named for its intense geothermal activities within
its boundaries. It is a beautiful scenery including plumes of geothermal steam,
towering cliffs and gorges.
It is an ideal venue fro visitord to enjoy mountain biking, rock climbing and natural spa.

Lake Nakuru


The lake is at the bottom of rift valley. It has the beautiful sceneries of escarpments,
flamingos and has a diverse grassland vegetation. Creates a great site for picnincs,
bird watching and game drives.

Mount Kenya


The mount Kenya is the second largest in Africa.
Its breath taking glacier, tarns, lakes and springs makes
it a site to admire. Its plains are good for
camping and one can also go mountain climbing.

Lamu Island


As most focus is driven on the interior we cannot forget the coastal area of Kenya.
The lamu island is just one of the many tourist attraction around the coast.
It is sorrounded by indian ocean and is known for for its great culture heritage.
Its also for its warm sandy beaches.
Tourist can go for sea diving and marine siting.
